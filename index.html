About us

What is MonthlyBake?

Easy

Feel like baking, but don't feel like spending hundreds of dollars on new equipment and large quantity ingredients for a single recipe? Along with easy to follow recipes, MonthlyBake will provide all ingredients required. MonthlyBake makes it easy to satisfy your inner baker with new delicious recipes each month!

Quality

MonthlyBake sends delicious fail-proof recipes and quality ingredients straight to your door each month. All of our recipes have been tried and tested in our MonthlyBake kitchens by our amazing team. We promise nothing but the best!

Flexible

Want more than recipe per month? Less? We have multiple plans to choose from! Our team will help you find a plan that fits your budget and schedule!
